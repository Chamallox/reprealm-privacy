RepRealm Privacy Policy

Last updated 14 July 2026

RepRealm does not collect your personal data.

Camera

The camera counts your reps on your device. Nothing is recorded, stored, or uploaded. You can turn camera access off anytime in Settings.

Your data

Game progress stays on your device. Purchases are handled by Apple. We do not collect your name, email, location, or any advertising or health data.
