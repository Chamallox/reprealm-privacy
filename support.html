Fitness RPG

RepRealm turns real push-ups into an RPG: every rep you do in front of your camera powers your hero through a ladder of bosses. Here is how to get help.

Common questions

Reps are not counting.

Make sure camera access is on (Settings > RepRealm > Camera), keep your whole upper body in frame, and use a well-lit spot. Front camera works best propped at floor level.

I paid but do not see premium.

Open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases. Restores use the Apple ID you bought with.

How do I manage or cancel a subscription?

In your device Settings, tap your name > Subscriptions, then choose RepRealm.

Need more help? Email francois@nomi.so and we will get back to you.
